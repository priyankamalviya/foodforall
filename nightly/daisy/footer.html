Footers

Footer types

You can have footer on all your pages. If you want fixed footer add data-position="fixed" into your footer markup. You can have different kinds of footers with JQM navbars. All types of navbars are available in JQM Docs page and are 100% compatibile with Daisy

Show source

<div data-role="footer" data-position="fixed" data-theme="none" data-border="false">
    <div data-role="navbar">
        <ul>
            <li><a href="mailto:email@email"><i class="fa fa-envelope"></i>Mail us</a></li>
            <li><a href="tel:0000000000"><i class="fa fa-phone"></i>Call us</a></li>
            <li><a href="#"><i class="fa fa-pencil"></i>Chat with us</a></li>
        </ul>
    </div>
</div>
